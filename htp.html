Hit The Bubble

Know How To Play!

Dive into a mesmerizing computer/mobile bubble game! Hit bubbles, Make Scores, and embark on a colorful journey. Relaxing gameplay with challenging levels awaits. Experience endless fun on your fingertips/Cursors!

In this game, a random number will generate automatically in "Hit Box" and Once you hit that numbered bubble by clicking on it, your score will get increased by +10, and bubbles will get re-arranged automatically and a New Hit Will Get Generate.
